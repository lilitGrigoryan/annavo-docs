Annavo is a mobile marketplace application where users can buy and sell items across multiple categories. Whether you're looking to post announcements, browse listings, or communicate with other users, Annavo makes it easy to connect with buyers and sellers in your community.

## Account & Registration

### How do I create an account?

1. Download the Annavo app from the App Store or Google Play
2. Tap "Register"
3. Enter your email address or username
4. Create a secure password
5. Verify your email
6. Complete your profile (optional)

### I forgot my password. How can I reset it?

1. On the login screen, tap "Forgot Password?"
2. Enter your email address
3. You'll receive a password reset link via email
4. Click the link and create a new password
5. Log in with your new password

### How do I update my profile?

1. Go to Settings in the app
2. Tap "Account" or "Profile"
3. Update your information (name, phone, location, etc.)
4. Add or change your profile picture
5. Save your changes

### How do I delete my account?

1. Go to Settings
2. Scroll to the bottom and find "Delete Account"
3. Confirm your decision
4. Enter your password to verify
5. Your account and associated data will be permanently deleted

## Posting & Announcements

### How do I post an announcement?

1. Tap the "+" or "Create" button
2. Select a category
3. Add a title and description
4. Add photos (optional)
5. Set a price (optional)
6. Select your location
7. Tap "Post"

### What makes a good announcement?

- Clear, descriptive title
- Detailed description of the item
- Clear, well-lit photos from multiple angles
- Accurate price
- Specific location information
- Honest condition description (new, like new, good, fair)
- Contact information or preference for communication

### Can I edit my announcement after posting?

Yes! Go to "My Announcements" or "My Posts", find the announcement, and tap "Edit". You can update the title, description, price, photos, and other details at any time.

### How long does my announcement stay posted?

Your announcements remain posted indefinitely unless you choose to delete or mark them as sold. We recommend regularly updating your listing or marking items as sold once you've completed the transaction.

### What shouldn't I post on Annavo?

Do not post:
- Illegal items or services
- Hate speech or discriminatory content
- Items that infringe on intellectual property
- Stolen goods
- Services that are illegal in your area
- Contact information in the photos (phone numbers, addresses)
- Multiple identical listings (spam)

**Violations may result in account suspension or termination.**

## Transactions & Communication

### How do I contact a seller/buyer?

1. Open the announcement you're interested in
2. Tap "Contact Seller" or "Send Message"
3. Start a conversation
4. Exchange details for meeting or payment arrangements

All communication happens within the Annavo app to protect both parties' privacy.

### Does Annavo handle payments?

No, Annavo does not process payments directly. Payment arrangements are made between the buyer and seller. We recommend using secure payment methods such as bank transfers, PayPal, or meeting in person with cash. Always verify the item before making payment.

### What if I don't receive my item after payment?

Since Annavo doesn't handle payments, we recommend:

1. Only use payment methods that offer buyer protection (PayPal, credit cards)
2. Use video call to verify the item before sending money
3. Meet in person whenever possible
4. Get written confirmation of details
5. Report fraudulent users through the app

If fraud occurs, report it to your payment provider and to local authorities.

### Can I leave a rating or review?

Yes! After completing a transaction, you can rate and review the other user. This helps build trust in the community and helps others make informed decisions. Ratings are based on factors like communication, honesty, and follow-through.

## Safety & Security

### How can I stay safe when using Annavo?

**When Meeting in Person:**
- Meet in a public, well-lit location
- Tell a friend/family member where you're going
- Bring someone with you if possible
- Trust your instincts
- Inspect the item before payment
- Avoid carrying large amounts of cash

**Online Safety:**
- Never share personal information (address, phone) in messages
- Use secure payment methods
- Verify user profiles and ratings
- Report suspicious behavior immediately

### What should I do if I encounter suspicious activity?

1. Do not proceed with the transaction
2. Do not share personal information
3. Take screenshots of the conversation
4. Tap the user's profile and select "Report" or "Block"
5. Email us at support with details: [lilit.grigoryan009@gmail.com](mailto:lilit.grigoryan009@gmail.com)
6. Report to local authorities if you believe a crime has occurred

### Is my personal information secure?

Yes, we use industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your data. Your password is securely hashed, and we never sell your information to third parties. For details, see our Privacy Policy.

### How do I block a user?

1. Go to the conversation or user profile
2. Tap the three dots (⋯) or menu icon
3. Select "Block User"
4. Confirm your action

Once blocked, the user cannot message you or see your announcements.

## Technical Issues

### The app won't load. What should I do?

Try these steps:

1. Close the app completely
2. Restart your device
3. Check your internet connection
4. Update the app from App Store/Google Play
5. Clear the app cache (Settings → Apps → Annavo → Storage → Clear Cache)
6. Reinstall the app if nothing else works

If the problem persists, email us at [lilit.grigoryan009@gmail.com](mailto:lilit.grigoryan009@gmail.com)

### Why can't I upload photos?

Check:

1. You have granted camera/photo permissions (Settings → Apps → Annavo → Permissions)
2. You have sufficient storage space on your device
3. Your internet connection is stable
4. The image format is supported (JPEG, PNG)
5. The image file size is reasonable (under 5MB recommended)

Try restarting the app and try again.

### The app is running slow. How can I speed it up?

1. Clear app cache (Settings → Apps → Annavo → Storage → Clear Cache)
2. Close other running apps
3. Check your internet connection
4. Update the app to the latest version
5. Restart your device
6. Consider freeing up storage space on your device

### Which devices/OS versions are supported?

**iOS:** iOS 13.0 or later  
**Android:** Android 8.0 or later

For the best experience, we recommend updating to the latest available version of your device's operating system.
